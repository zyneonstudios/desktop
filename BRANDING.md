# Branding and Trademark Policy

This document defines how the Zyneon Desktop name, logo, and brand may be used.

## Project Name

The name `Zyneon Desktop` identifies the original project maintained by the
original authors and contributors. Forks and derivative works may describe
themselves as forks of Zyneon Desktop, but must not present themselves as the
official project unless explicitly authorized.

## Trademarks and Visual Identity

Unless a separate written permission is granted, derivative works may not use:
- the Zyneon Desktop name in a way that implies endorsement, sponsorship, or
  official affiliation
- official logos, icons, wordmarks, or product branding
- confusingly similar names or artwork that could mislead users into believing
  the fork is the original project

## Allowed Referencing

Forks may use factual, nominative references such as:
- "fork of Zyneon Desktop"
- "based on Zyneon Desktop"
- "compatible with Zyneon Desktop"

Those references must be accurate and must not imply that the fork is the
official release.

## Required Attribution

Derivative works should preserve visible attribution to the original authors
where practical, and must preserve copyright and license notices required by the
project license.

## No Endorsement

Do not use the project name, branding, or contributor identities to imply
endorsement unless you have explicit permission from the original maintainers.

## Enforcement

This policy is separate from the software license. A license may permit copying,
modification, and redistribution, but it does not automatically grant trademark
or branding rights.

Questions about use of the name or branding should be directed to the project
maintainers before publication.
